How to Play CornShark

The object of the game is to grow to Level 16, where you're the biggest fish in the sea. The green bar at the top of the screen shows your progress toward this goal.

  • Use the arrow keys to move the CornShark.
  • Attack other fish by running your mouth into them. The CornShark will bite automatically when his face hits another fish.
  • When you eat enough fish, you increase in size and strength. The yellow bar at the top of the screen shows your progress toward this.
  • Try to avoid getting bit by the other fish. They can shrink and weaken you. If you lose all your health at Level 0, it's game over!
  • If a fish has sparkling particles trailing from him, he's a power-up and will enhance the CornShark in some way. Go get him!
  • The Escape key pauses the game and brings up a menu.
