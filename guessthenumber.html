History:

This program, as recommended by our textbook, was given as a final exam objective to some of my Java classes. I developed it myself to show them an example of what I expected them to do, and how I would be testing and grading their work. I also frequently use it to demonstrate the logic behing the binary search algorithm.
Motivation:

Aside from wanting it as an example, there really wasn't a technical motivation for this project. It was mere minutes' worth of work, so there's really not much to say about it.
